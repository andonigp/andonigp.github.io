Data

Pyhton Preprocess

The data used for this visualization belongs to the Tripadvisor's europe restaurants dataset. The dataset was processed with python Pandas library to extract only the data required for this visualization.

Visualization

Tableau

The visualization dashboard was configured in Tableau Public.

The idea is to have a dashboard in which you may find the adecuate restaurant in Catalunya, according to your needs. The filters that may help you find the restaurant you are looking for are:

  • Cuisines
  • Province
  • Price Level
  • Gluten free
  • Vegan Options

The down part of the dashboard gives the list of restaurants filtered, the address and the rating given to the restaurant by the Tripadvisor users. Once you have selected one restaurant on the map, the dashboard will display its Tripadvisor webpage for further information and references.
